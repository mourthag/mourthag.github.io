Welcome

Welcome to my Homepage. My name is Julian and I'm a software developer who recently graduated with a Master's degree from Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT). My main topic of interest is image synthesis and GPGPU in which I specialized at KIT through various labs, seminars and thesis'. I also gathered some practical experience with deep learning and computer vision at KIT.

Projects

Here is a selected List of some of my projects:

  • Unity Path Tracer
    A real-time path tracer implemented in compute shaders for Unity with Next Event Estimation, Disney BSDF and BVH generation.

  • Discord Raid Planner
    A discord bot that helps scheduling raids with friends. Mainly used for Destiny 2 due to the default activities available.

  • Non-Overlapped Tiling for Spatiotemporal Variance-Guided Filtering
    This project was the topic of my masters thesis. I optimized SVGF, a real-time denoising filter for path traced images, by implementing a new scheduling method for its iterative spatial filter. The rescheduling was based on polyhedral optimization, i.e. Split Tiling, and achieved a speedup of factor two, halving the overall runtime.

  • Fast Volumetric Obscurance with Moment-based Reconstructions
    This project was the topic of my bachelors thesis. I implemented a new screen-space approach for volumetric obscurance, the general version of ambient occlusion, that uses several moments of the depth buffer combined with a spatial blur. The results were promising but still featured several artifacts and the runtime was limited due to the expensive spatial blur.
